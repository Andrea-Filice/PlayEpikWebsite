CUBE INVASION.

A 'simple' tower defense game.









A SHORT DESCRIPTION

Cube Invasion is a Tower Defense game, the aim of the game is to buy with money (earned by killing enemy cubes that try to attack you) all the available towers! The towers will shoot at the enemy closest to them and try to kill him, but you can help them by clicking on the enemy cube to take life!

WHERE CAN I PLAY IT?

Not avaible for smartphones.
