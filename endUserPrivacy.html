0. UPDATES AND PATCHES
Play Epik's video games receive updates to fix in-game problems or security patches on their servers, which is why we would like to better explain how to update our video games to the latest version in the right way, and we will outline the steps below.

1. Visit our itch or gamejolt site and download the right package for your computer, to see if your machine has a 64 or 32 bit processor: WINDOWS 11/10 : Device Settings > System > Go down to the bottom and find the ‘system information’ > find the ‘System Type’ entry and that's where you can find out if your computer has a 64 or 32 bit processor.

2. If you have installed another version of the product, first uninstall the previous version and then start the installer of the new version.

3. If no previous version has been installed, you can proceed by opening the terminal named (‘SetupX’ or ‘GameNameSetupX’) + device bit, following the information on the screen to install the video game.
