RUNNERS.

A game all to... jump.










A SHORT DESCRIPTION

Runners is a Platform game where the goal is to avoid falling through the ground and get to the end! The player's speed will increase as you progress through the map to make the game more difficult! If you fall before the end, you will be given rewards: Experience Points (To level up) and COINS (To buy another skin for 10,000!). If you get to the end, congratulations, you WON! You will receive the same rewards but in greater quantities!

WHERE CAN I PLAY IT?

Not avaible for smartphones.
