TASKIFY BUSINESS

Latest Version:


DESCRIPTION

Taskify Business is a powerful desktop application designed to help businesses organize, assign, and track maintenance tasks for their computer softwares. By providing a clear, centralized interface, Maintenance Scheduler streamlines planning, reduces downtime, and ensures each component receives timely updates and checks.


  TASK CATEGORIES

Categorize your activity. Select from 2 categories available, "Maintenance Task" or out.

  VERSION TRACKING

Keep track of newer version of tools and other third-softwares. Don't forgot to update it.

  POWERED BY AI

Create, Delete and Modify Tasks, only with a few commands ahead.

  PROGRESS DASHBOARD

Keep track of tasks and time at the same time. Simply with a graph.
